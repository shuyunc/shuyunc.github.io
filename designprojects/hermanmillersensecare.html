This careU system helps remote workers to maintain a work and life balance. It uses mouse and laptop as mediums to collect user's activity. As a result, personalized care reminders and daily work and life balance report will help user to feel uplifted and more productive.

Problem Statement

Remote workers are worried about not having a work and life balance while working from home because it is difficult to transition from work mode to relaxing mode within the same space.

Provisional Persona
“I wish I can maintain healthy habits when I work from home”

As our target user uses mouse heavily in their daily work, the mouse not only records user's productivity, but can also detect their emotional states. These information would help us to remind user to take care of themselves in the way they wanted.

Reflection

Since this is a concept project based on a provisional persona, I spend most of my time on how to create a system to satisfy my user's need of maintaining a work and life balance.
I started by ideation, there I came up with the idea of building a system around the mouse, as it is a device my user will use a lot on a daily basis. Then I build the prototype for my mouse design.

It was such a fun experience to design a product end to end with both physical and digital interaction. It allows me to think out of the box and come up with creative solutions!
